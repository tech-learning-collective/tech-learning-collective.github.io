Hacker Trivia Night

Hack the planet. Frag the man. Free the world.

Hacker Trivia Night is a cybersecurity competition that anyone can play to benefit local community beneficiaries.

Form a team with your friends, declare your beneficiary, and log on when the game starts to win cash prizes from a pool of donations that goes to support those in need.

Hacker Trivia Night is a Tech Learning Collective and Shift-CTRL Space production.
