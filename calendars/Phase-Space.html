Phase Space

Phase Space is a DIY makerspace, multimedia art studio, and experimental collective based in Brooklyn, NY, located near the Wilson Avenue [L] Subway station in the Bushwick neighborhood.

We are dedicated to the exploration of video, sound, performance, creative coding, math + science, and interdisciplinary art practices.
