Export Event Calendar

See all Tech Learning Collective events in your (Google/Apple/Microsoft/Mozilla/etcetera) calendar app! Simply click the link below:

Subscribe to our calendar.

If clicking the link does not open your calendaring application, it means your Web browser does not have a handler associated with the webcal scheme. You can either add this yourself, or simply use the Web address of our events’ iCalendar feed directly in your calendaring application by changing webcal to https in the URL:

https://techlearningcollective.com/events/all.ics

Your calendaring application will also have instructions in its user manual for subscribing to calendars like ours. For example:

  • Google Calendar Support
  • Apple Calendar Support
  • Microsoft Outlook Support
  • Mozilla Thunderbird Lightning Calendar Support
  • Nextcloud Calendar documentation

Alternatively, you can subscribe to the Atom feed for our events. If you use a news feed reader capable of consuming Atom feeds (all modern ones are), a new article will be added to this feed with the event details when we publish an event on our Web site.

Subscribing to one of our event feeds linked above guarantees that you won’t miss your chance to participate in our free, by-donation, and low-cost public events.
