Donate to Tech Learning Collective

Tech Learning Collective graciously accepts in-kind donations in any amount in support of our work offering unparalleled free, by-donation, and low-cost computer classes to people of marginalized groups and politically engaged individuals in need. Your donation helps ensure we can continue developing best-in-class educational material on topics ranging from fundamental computer literacy to information and digital security, network engineering, system administration, Web design and development, and more, as well as continue to support community-owned technology projects with our partner and sister organizations.
